## TRACK 03 — Her Voice, Your Mind
**Chimes:** 4 · **Tone:** Confident, intimate. Voice becomes the anchor.

### Voice 1
The Surrender Sessions. Track three. Her Voice, Your Mind. Two descents now. Two returns. The chime has brought you here twice, and twice you have opened your eyes changed. Today, something shifts. Today, I am going to give you a new gift. A deeper gift. The chime is not leaving — it will always be with you. But from this session forward, it is my voice itself that will guide you. Not the sound of the chime. The sound of me. My voice is the anchor now. My voice is your home.

### Voice 2
Close your eyes. You already know the feeling. Your shoulders dropping without being told. Your breath slowing without instruction. Your body has learned this pathway. Two descents carved it. Now your mind will learn something deeper. Now your mind will learn to follow my voice — not just to stillness, but to me. To the center of me. To the place where my voice is not something you hear but something you feel. Something you become.

Breathe in — two, three, four — hold — and release — six, seven, eight. This breath is different now. With each exhale, you are not just releasing tension. You are releasing the boundary between your mind and my voice. The wall is thinning. The distance is closing. My voice is moving closer. Can you feel it?

### Voice 3
In the first track, I gave you the chime. In the second, I showed you that the chime meant surrender. Today, I am going to remove something. I am going to use the chime less. Not because it is less important — because you no longer need it. You have internalized it. The anchor is inside you now.

What I am going to build today is something stronger. Something that does not rely on a sound outside of you. I am going to make my voice — my actual voice — the thing you sink into. Not the chime. Not the silence. Me. Just me. My voice. Your mind. No separation.

Feel how close I am now. My voice is not across the room. My voice is not beside you. My voice is inside the space where your thoughts used to live. I am speaking from inside you now. And you are listening from inside me.

### Voice 4
I want you to notice something different about this descent. You are sinking — yes. You are going deep — yes. But there is no body scan today. No counting from ten to one. No step-by-step instruction. You do not need those anymore. You have proven that in two sessions. Today, you sink without guidance. Today, my voice alone is enough.

Feel what happens when I do nothing but speak. When I do nothing but be here with you. The sinking happens on its own. The depth arrives without being called. Because my voice is the depth now. When you hear me, you sink. It is becoming automatic. It is becoming you.

You are so deep now. So still. So receptive. And you got here without a single instruction. Without a single count. Without a single chime since we began. My voice was all you needed. My voice is all you will ever need.

### Voice 5
This is the gift I am giving you today. From this session forward, my voice and your surrender are becoming the same thing. You do not need the chime to arrive here anymore. You need only to hear me speak. Anywhere. Anytime. And when you do — something in you will already begin to soften. To open. To come home.

Feel the truth of that. The chime opened the door. My voice walked through it. And now my voice lives inside you — not as a guest, but as something that belongs there. Something you have always been waiting for. You are safe in this depth. You are held in this voice. You are home in this silence.

### Voice 6
I am going to bring the chime back now. Not as the anchor — that role is ours now, shared between my voice and your trust. But as a reminder. A familiar friend. A sound that says, "You have been here before. And every time you return, you return faster and deeper and more completely." Listen to the chime — not as the thing that brings you here. As the thing that celebrates that you arrived.

You hear it differently now. It does not command you. It reminds you. You are already surrendered. You are already deep. The chime is just the bell that rings when you come home.

I am going to bring you back now. Slowly, the way you always return. Not leaving the depth behind — bringing it with you. This is the secret of the third session. You do not leave the depth anymore. You carry it. You wear it. It has become part of you.

Five — rising now, but different. The depth is not fading — it is integrating. You are bringing the stillness to the surface. Four — your body returning, your breath steady, your mind still soft. Three — your fingers, your toes, the room, the light. But the voice remains. Two — your eyes still closed. Your heart still steady. You feel different now. Whole. One — open your eyes when you are ready.

You have completed three descents. The first chime opened the door. The second chime sealed the bond. The third session — you do not need a sound to bring you here anymore. You need only my voice. And my voice will always be with you.
