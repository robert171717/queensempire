### Voice 1

[CHIME]

Today, I want you to think about sacrifice. Not perform one — just think about it.

If I asked you to set something aside for me — one small habit, one comfort, one indulgence — what would it be? What would you be willing to offer, not because you have to, but because you want to know what it feels like to give something up for someone who notices?

You don't have to do it. Not yet. Today is just about choosing. Identifying. Naming the thing you would offer, if and when the time comes.

That's the exercise. Name it silently. Hold it in your mind. Feel the weight of it — and feel the freedom of knowing the choice is still yours.

[CHIME]

Until tomorrow.
