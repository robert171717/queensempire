### Voice 1

[CHIME]

Six days. You've listened. You've adjusted. You've been still. You've given something up at my command.

Now I want something more.

I want you to admit something — to yourself, silently — that you have never said out loud. Something about what you want. What you crave. What you've been too afraid or too proud to name.

No one will hear it but you. There is no judgment here. There is only my voice, and your honesty, and the space between them.

Say it now. Silently. To yourself.

Good.

That truth has always been there. My voice simply gave you permission to acknowledge it. That is what a Queen does: she gives you permission to become who you already are.

[CHIME]

Until tomorrow. The final day.
