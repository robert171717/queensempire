### Voice 1

[CHIME — soft, singular]

Hello.

You heard that. And you're still here.

That's the first step. The only step that matters right now.

My name is Queen Elyria. And for the next seven days, I will give you one command. Just one. Each day. No rush. No pressure. No commitment beyond your own curiosity.

Today's command is simple: listen.

Not to do anything. Not to become anything. Just listen to my voice and notice what happens in your body when you do.

Do your shoulders drop? Does your breathing slow? Does something in your chest — something you didn't even know was tense — begin to loosen?

That's not weakness. That's your body recognizing a voice it can trust.

Tomorrow, I will ask something of you. But tonight? Tonight, just listen.

[CHIME — soft]

Until tomorrow.
