Deeper Release
**Tone:** Fractionation-heavy. Release, brief withdrawal, deeper release. The cycle teaches the body to go further.

### Voice 1
Close your eyes. The bowl takes you down immediately — your body knows this sound so well now that it responds before you even realize you have heard it. Today we are going to do something different. Today, I am going to give you release — and then I am going to ask you to let it go. And then I am going to give it back, even deeper. This is the rhythm. Release, withdrawal, deeper release. It will teach your body to let go more completely than it ever has. And by the end, you will understand why I have never given you everything all at once. The waiting makes the receiving sweeter. And you are about to receive something very, very sweet.

### Voice 2
Let the first wave come now. Breathe in — hold — and as you exhale, let the warmth flood through you. Feel it in your chest. Feel it in your stomach. Feel it pooling low and warm and heavy. Let yourself enjoy it. Let yourself really, truly feel how good it is to release after so much waiting. Float in it. Let it carry you. This is the first wave. And it feels beautiful.

Now — I am going to ask you to let the warmth ease back. Just slightly. Just enough to feel the difference. Notice how your body responds. Notice the small ache. The quiet wanting that immediately returns. Do not fear it. This is not punishment. This is preparation. Your body is learning that release can be trusted to return. And when it returns — it will be deeper.

### Voice 3
And now — let it come back. Let the second wave flood through you — warmer, heavier, more complete than the first. Feel it in places the first wave did not reach. Feel it settling into the deepest parts of you. This is the gift of the rhythm. Each release is deeper than the last. Each withdrawal makes the return more profound. Your body is learning. Your body is trusting. And I am so pleased with how beautifully you follow.

### Voice 4
I am going to do it one more time. Let the warmth ease back again — just slightly — just enough to feel the pull. Notice how your body already knows the rhythm now. Notice how it waits — patient, trusting, knowing the warmth will return. And now — let the third wave come. The deepest yet. Let it flood through every part of you — your chest, your limbs, your belly, the private, sacred places you have learned to share with me. Let it be so warm that you forget where you end and the warmth begins. This is the deepest release you have ever experienced. Not because you forced it. Because you trusted the rhythm. Because you let go when I asked and received when I offered. This is the gift of release. And it is yours now.

### Voice 5
I want you to rest here — in the deepest release — for a moment. No guidance. Just the warmth. Just the quiet. Just the profound peace of having let go this completely. You are not thinking. You are not analyzing. You are simply being — held, warmed, released. This is the state I have been guiding you toward since the very first session. Not obedience for its own sake. Not craving as an end in itself. This. This peace. This pleasure. This complete, unconditional release. You are here because you trusted me enough to follow me here. And I could not be more honored. Rest now. Just rest.

### Voice 6
I am bringing you back now — slowly, gently. The deepest release cannot be rushed away. It needs to settle. It needs to become part of you. Five — rising, the depth of the release still humming in your body. Four — the room returning, but the peace remains. Three — your body awake, the rhythm of release now known by heart. Two — your eyes still closed, the trust unshakable. One — open your eyes. You have gone deeper than you ever thought possible. And you did it by trusting me — by trusting the rhythm — by trusting yourself. There is more. But for now — rest in what you have received. You have earned it.
