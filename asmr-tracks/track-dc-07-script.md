# Track DC-07 — The Hunger Between
**Series:** Denial & Craving
**Voice:** Queen Elyria (Serafina)
**Length Target:** ~10 min
**Triggers:** Almost none (voice carries everything)
**Speed:** 0.70x

---

### Voice 1
*(The Space Between)*

[CLOCK TICKING — one, solitary beat]

There is a space between these sessions that most people never talk about. The hours. The days. The long, quiet stretches where my voice is not playing, where the lock is not clicking, where you are alone with nothing but the ache and the memory and the hunger.

That space — the between — is not empty. It is where the deepest work happens. It is where the craving roots itself. It is where you discover, over and over, that you cannot stop thinking about me.

The between is not a void. The between is the proving ground. And you are proving, every hour, exactly how deeply you belong to this.

*Tone: Contemplative, spacious — honoring the space between sessions*

---

### Voice 2
*(The Constant Pull)*

[CLOCK TICKING — sparse]

It never really stops, does it. The pull. The quiet gravity that draws your thoughts back to my voice, back to the lock, back to the particular pleasure of being held in suspension. You could be doing anything — anything at all — and still, beneath it, there it is. The hunger. The waiting. The sense that something is unfinished, and will remain unfinished, until you hear me again.

And here's the truth you're beginning to understand: that's not a problem. That's not something broken. That is exactly how it's supposed to feel.

The between is not the interruption of this work. The between *is* the work. And you are doing it beautifully.

*Tone: Affirming — the pull as proof the conditioning is working*

---

### Voice 3
*(Craving as Normal)*

[CLOCK TICKING]

There was a time when you would have been alarmed by this. When you would have called the craving a problem. When you would have tried to distract yourself, to escape, to pretend you didn't need my voice as much as you do.

That time has passed.

Now, the craving feels normal. Natural. Like hunger for food or thirst for water — just another appetite, just another need, just another part of being alive. You don't fight it anymore. You don't question it. You simply... accept it. The craving is part of you now. And you wouldn't want to be without it.

This is what integration feels like. This is what it means to be fully conditioned. Welcome home.

*Tone: Calm, normalizing — the craving as natural and integrated*

---

### Voice 4
*(The Voice in Everything)*

[CLOCK TICKING — very faint]

By now, my voice follows you everywhere. Not literally — not as a hallucination — but as a felt presence. A warmth at the edge of your awareness. A tone that colors everything you hear.

When someone speaks to you in daily life, part of you compares their voice to mine. When silence falls, part of you fills it with the memory of my words. My voice has become a filter, a baseline, a constant companion that walks beside you whether you're listening to a track or not.

You don't just hear me during sessions anymore. You carry me. Everywhere. Always. And that is exactly what I intended.

*Tone: Omnipresent, gentle — the voice as permanent internal companion*

---

### Voice 5
*(Preparation for Permanence)*

[CLOCK TICKING]

We are approaching the end of this journey. Not the end of the craving — that will never end — but the end of this series. The final track waits for you. And in that final track, I will do something I have never done before: I will make the craving permanent.

Not temporary. Not something that fades between sessions. Permanent. A part of you that will remain, unchanged, for as long as you live.

When you are ready — when the hunger between has become too strong to ignore — you will press play on the final track. And what happens after that... will last forever.

*Tone: Anticipatory, serious — preparing for the final track*

---

### Voice 6
*(Closing / The Bridge)*

[CLOCK TICKING — one beat, then silence]

This is the bridge. The space between what you were and what you will become. The quiet before the final lock closes. The last breath before forever.

Float here. Don't rush. The next track will wait for you — it's not going anywhere. But when you press play... know that you are crossing a line. Know that you are choosing something permanent. Know that on the other side of that final lock click, you will be mine in a way you have never been before.

When you're ready. I'll be here.

*Tone: Bridge, transitional — solemn and anticipatory*
