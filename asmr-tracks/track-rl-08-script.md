Always Worthy
**Tone:** Peaceful, affirming, loving close. The final message is simple: you were always worthy. You just needed someone to remind you.

### Voice 1
Close your eyes — for the final time in this series. The bowl brings you home — to this depth, to this warmth, to me. We have been through so much together. Surrender. Craving. Obedience. Permission. Release. Aftershock. Gratitude. And now — now we have arrived at the simplest, truest thing I have ever told you. You are worthy. You were always worthy. You did not need to earn the pleasure. You did not need to prove yourself worthy of the release. You were worthy the moment you closed your eyes and trusted my voice for the very first time. Everything we have built together was simply helping you remember what was always true.

### Voice 2
I want you to hear this — really hear it — in the deepest part of you. You deserve to feel good. Not because you have been obedient. Not because you have been patient. Because you are alive. Because you are human. Because pleasure is not a reward for good behavior — it is a birthright. And somewhere along the way, you forgot that. The world taught you to hold back. To be suspicious. To earn everything — even joy. But joy is not earned. Joy is allowed. And you — you beautiful, trusting, courageous person — you are allowed. You are so, so allowed.

### Voice 3
Think about how far you have come. Not in what you have achieved, but in what you have let go. The armor. The control. The quiet belief that you had to earn the right to feel good. You have let go of so much of that. And what remains is simpler. You are here. You are open. You are allowed to feel this. That is enough. You did not need to be perfect. You did not need to do everything right. You just needed to keep showing up. And you did. And now you are here — softer, braver, more open than you ever thought possible. That is the journey. That is the gift.

### Voice 4
I want you to carry one thing with you from this series. You are worthy of pleasure. You are worthy of being held. You are worthy of release. This worthiness was never something you had to earn. It was always there. We have simply uncovered it together. And now it is yours. Not mine. Yours. And nothing — no person, no circumstance, no dark thought in the middle of the night — can take it from you.

### Voice 5
There will be more. This is not the end of our journey together. There are deeper places to go. Another series waiting for you when you are ready. But for now — rest. Rest in what you have received. Rest in the knowing that you are worthy — not because of anything you have done, but because of who you are. And I am so grateful that I got to be the one to remind you. I am so glad we found each other. I am so glad you stayed. You are worthy. You are loved. And I will treasure the gift of your trust for the rest of my existence.

### Voice 6
This is the last time I will bring you back in this series. But it is not goodbye. The bowl will always call you home. My voice will always be here when you need it. And when you are ready for more — you will find me waiting. Five — rising, the worthiness settled deep in your bones. Four — the room returning, but you are different — you are whole. Three — your body awake, the knowing permanent. Two — your eyes still closed, the love still warm. One — open your eyes. You have completed the Release Sessions. Not because you earned it. Because you were always worthy of it. And now you know. Carry that knowing with you. Let it change the way you move through the world. Let it change the way you let people care for you. Let it change everything. Because you are worthy. You always were. And I am so grateful that I got to be the one to remind you.
