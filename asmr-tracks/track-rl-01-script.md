The Permission
**Tone:** Warm, almost emotional. After all this time, she is finally giving permission. Relief.

### Voice 1
Close your eyes. The bowl brings you down — but today, something is different. Today, the descent feels lighter. Less like sinking. More like being lowered into something warm and waiting. You have been so patient. You have surrendered. You have craved. You have obeyed. And now — now I am going to give you something you have been wanting for a very long time. Permission. Permission to let go completely. Permission to feel everything. Permission to release. Not because you earned it — though you have. Because you are ready. And I am so proud of how far you have come.

### Voice 2
Take a breath in — slow and deep — hold it — and let it go. Feel how different that exhale feels today. It is not just breath leaving your body. It is tension. It is waiting. It is the quiet, patient hunger that has been living in your chest for so many sessions. You have been holding so much. Wanting so much. And I have asked you to wait. And you waited. You waited beautifully. You did not demand. You did not rush. You trusted that when the time was right, I would give you what you needed. The time is right. I am going to give you what you have been needing. And it is going to feel so much better because you waited for it.

### Voice 3
The permission I am giving you today is simple but real. You are allowed to feel good — fully, deeply, without needing to monitor or apologize for it. You do not need to hold back here. You do not need to question whether this is too much. It is exactly right. You are exactly right. And for the rest of this series, you have my full permission to receive whatever your body wants to receive. There is no wrong way to receive what I am about to give you. There is only your way. And your way is beautiful.

### Voice 4
I want you to locate the wanting in your body right now. Where does it live? Is it in your chest — a tightness, a warmth, a pressure? Is it lower — a pooling, a heaviness, a gentle ache? Is it everywhere — a full-body hunger that you have learned to live with but never learned to satisfy? Find it. Let yourself really feel it. And now — I want you to imagine that wanting beginning to soften. Not disappear. Soften. Like ice becoming water. Like a fist becoming an open hand. The wanting is not going away. It is transforming into something you are finally allowed to feel fully. Breathe into that softening. Let it spread. Let it become the first real letting go you have allowed yourself in a very long time.

### Voice 5
You have been so good. So patient. So willing to follow my voice through surrender, through craving, through obedience — all without the release your body has been quietly asking for. I know it has not been easy. I know there were moments when the wanting felt like too much. But you stayed. You trusted. You kept coming back. And now, the waiting is over. Not because I am done guiding you. Not because the journey is ending. Because you have reached a place where release will not undo everything we have built. Release will deepen it. Release will prove to your body that everything I have asked of you was leading somewhere beautiful. And it was. It truly was.

### Voice 6
I am bringing you back now — but you are carrying something new with you. Permission. It is not heavy. It is light. It is warm. It is the quiet knowing that the next time you close your eyes and hear the bowl, you will be allowed to receive. Fully. Completely. Without holding back. Five — rising, the permission settling into your heart. Four — the room returning, but you feel lighter. Three — your body awake, the permission yours now. Two — your eyes still closed, the anticipation sweet instead of aching. One — open your eyes. You have been given permission. Not to do anything differently in your life. To feel. To receive. To let pleasure be a part of this. The next session will be the first release. And it will be worth every moment you waited.
