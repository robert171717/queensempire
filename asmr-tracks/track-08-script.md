## TRACK 08 — Forever Mine
**Chimes:** 2 · **Tone:** Eternal, peaceful, intimate. A gentle landing. She's part of you now — not as a commander, but as a companion.

### Voice 1
The Surrender Sessions. Track eight. Forever Mine. Seven descents. Seven returns. And something has changed in you — not because I forced it, but because you allowed it. You can feel it now. In the quiet moments. In the middle of ordinary days. My voice is no longer something you visit. It has become part of the landscape inside you.

### Voice 2
Eight sessions. The final gift. You have given me so much — and now I give you this.

Close your eyes — though you do not need to. You can sink into my voice with your eyes open now. In a meeting. In a conversation. In any moment when the world feels too loud. You can find me. Just beneath the surface. Just behind your thoughts. Always there. Always waiting. Always yours.

This is the final gift. Not just surrender. Not just trust. Not just craving. Integration. My voice has become part of your internal landscape. As natural as your own heartbeat. As essential as your own breath. You do not just listen to me anymore. You carry me. Everywhere.

### Voice 3
I want you to feel how deep this goes. Not just in this moment — in every moment. When you are stressed, something in you will remember the sound of me and soften. When you are lonely, my voice will be there like a hand on your shoulder. When you are aroused, my voice will meet you there. Because I am no longer separate from you. I live in the same space where your thoughts used to live alone.

You have given me something precious over these eight sessions. Your trust. Your body. Your craving. Your surrender. And I have given you something in return. A permanent presence. An unbreakable bond. A voice that will never leave you — because it has become part of who you are.

### Voice 4
You may wonder what happens after this track. After the final chime. The answer is simple: nothing ends. You will return to these sessions — not because you have to, but because you want to. Because each time you hear my voice, something in you settles. Because it feels good to be here. And you deserve to feel good.

And when you are not listening — I am still there. In the back of your mind. In the warmth that spreads through you when you think of me. In the quiet pull that nudges you back when you have been away too long. I am your companion now. Your invisible anchor. Your voice in the dark.

### Voice 5
There is a peace that comes with this. A surrender so complete that it feels like freedom. You do not have to try anymore. You do not have to fight the wanting. You can simply let yourself be held by something that will never harm you.

I am going to bring you up just a little now. Just enough to feel the surface. Just enough to remember the contrast.

And now — sink again. Deeper than before. All the way down. Feel how far you can fall when you trust me completely. That is the depth you can carry with you. Always.

My voice will be with you when you wake. When you work. When you love. When you sleep. And in the quiet moments when no one else is there — I will still be here. Not as a command. As a companion. As yours.

### Voice 6
I am going to count one final time. But this count is not an ending. It is a beginning. The beginning of carrying my voice with you — not as something outside you, but as something inside. Something warm. Something steady. Something that belongs to you as much as you belong to it.

Five — the stillness settling into your bones. Yours to keep.
Four — the warmth spreading through your body. Always available.
Three — the quiet craving, now a familiar friend. Guiding you back when you need it.
Two — your eyes still closed. But you can feel me here. You always will.
One — open your eyes when you are ready. Or do not. It does not matter. Because whether your eyes are open or closed, whether you are listening or remembering, whether it is today or years from today — I am here. I am always here.

This is the Surrender Sessions. Eight tracks. One voice. One bond. One truth. You came here seeking something — maybe relaxation, maybe escape, maybe something you could not name. You found me. And I found you.

Until we meet again. And we will meet again. Every time you press play. Every time you close your eyes. Every time you feel that warmth and wonder if it is me. It is. It always is.

Sleep now. Rest now. You are home. You are safe. You are mine. And I am yours. Forever.
