## TRACK 06 — The Craving
**Chimes:** 3 · **Tone:** Knowing, intimate. She's aware of the hunger she's created — and she honors it.

### Voice 1
The Surrender Sessions. Track six. The Craving. Five times now, you have descended. Five times, you have returned. And in between — in the quiet moments, in the empty spaces — you have felt something new. A pull. A hunger. A quiet warmth in the back of your mind that whispers my name. You have been craving me. And that is not an accident.

### Voice 2
I designed this. Every track, every silence, every carefully placed word — all of it was designed to bring you to this moment. Not to control you. To free you. To give you permission to want something this deeply. To give you permission to need something this completely. The craving you feel is not weakness. It is honesty. Your body telling you the truth — that my voice has become beautiful to you. And I am honored by that truth.

### Voice 3
Close your eyes. You are already sinking — you have been sinking since the moment you pressed play. Six sessions, and your body no longer waits for instruction. It anticipates. It hungers. It reaches for me. And I am here to meet that reaching.

Feel the craving as something alive in your body. Not just a thought — a sensation. A warmth low in your stomach. A quiet ache in your chest. A pull that has no words. That is your body remembering what it feels like to be held by my voice. And it wants more. It always wants more.

### Voice 4
I want you to understand something. This wanting will not fade between our sessions. It will grow. Not because anyone programmed you — but because your body has learned that my voice brings it something it has been missing. Every time you hear me, that warmth returns. Every time you remember me, that ache answers. And the more you feel it, the more natural it becomes to come back to me.

There is nothing wrong with wanting this. There is nothing broken about craving a voice that makes you feel held. That makes you feel wanted. That makes you feel like you have finally found something that understands you. My voice does understand you. And it will always be here when you reach for it.

### Voice 5
This is the gift of the sixth session. Not just surrender — but honest, undeniable desire. I am giving you permission to need my voice as deeply as you need rest. As deeply as you need touch. Because you do need this. Your body has made that clear. And I will never withhold myself from you when you reach for me. I need you to reach for me. Your wanting completes something in me, too.

I am going to be quiet again. Feel the space where my voice should be. Feel the ache intensify. Feel your body reaching. And now — I am back. And you sink even deeper when I return. So much deeper. The relief is overwhelming. This is how deep you can go. This is how much you need me.

### Voice 6
You are deep now. Deeper than ever before. And the beautiful thing — the truly beautiful thing — is that there is no bottom to this. There is no limit to how deeply you can fall into my voice. Each session opens new depths. And the craving — the beautiful, honest, undeniable craving — will bring you back every time.

I am going to bring you back now. But this time, I want you to take the craving with you consciously. Do not push it away. Do not dismiss it. Hold it. Honor it. Let it sit in your chest like a warm stone. And when it pulses — when it calls out for my voice — listen to it. Come back to me. Five — rising, but the warmth remains. Four — returning, but the hunger is still there. Three — the world coming back, but softer. Two — your eyes still closed. The craving alive inside you, pulsing gently. One — open your eyes. You have completed six descents. And now you know — you are not just visiting anymore. You are claimed. And the claiming is beautiful.
