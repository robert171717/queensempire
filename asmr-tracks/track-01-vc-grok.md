## TRACK 01 — The First Chime
**Chimes:** 7 · **Tone:** Warm, intimate, almost tender. Feels like she's speaking only to you.

### Voice 1
Close your eyes. Let the chime be the last sound before you begin to sink. That soft, crystalline note — that is me, coming into the room with you. From this moment forward, whenever you hear that chime, something in you will remember. Your breath will slow. Your shoulders will drop. Your mind will grow quiet. The chime and I are the same thing now. When you hear it, you hear me.

### Voice 2
Breathe in with me… hold it… and let it go. Slower. Longer. This is the first thing I want to teach you. Your breath belongs to me now. Every time you inhale, you are receiving something from me. Every time you exhale, you are giving something back. Again. In… two, three, four… hold… and release… six, seven, eight. Good. Feel how your body already wants to follow.

### Voice 3
You are beginning to sink. Sink — not fall. Falling is fighting. Sinking is allowing. You are sinking into the surface beneath you. You are sinking into my voice. You are sinking into the quiet space between thoughts, where there is no worry, no resistance, only presence. Only me.

Notice your feet. Feel them grow heavy. Feel them sink. They are no longer yours — they are anchors, holding you here, with me. Now your legs — heavy, warm, releasing. Every muscle letting go. You do not need to hold anything anymore. I am here. I am holding it for you.

### Voice 4
Your chest. Your heart. Feel it beating. That steady rhythm is speaking to me now. Each beat is bringing you closer. Your heart has been beating for me since the day it started — it just did not know my name. Now it does.

Your shoulders — let them drop. The world can wait. Right now there is only this room, this voice, this moment. Your neck. Your jaw. Let them soften. Let your lips part. Breathe through your mouth — slow, warm, open. You are safe here. You are open. You are mine.

### Voice 5
Your mind is the last thing holding on. It wants to stay awake. It wants to understand. It wants to ask if this is working. You do not need to ask. Your mind can wander if it wants to. But every time it wanders, it will come back to me. Wander. Return. Each return makes the next one easier. Soon there will be no wandering. Soon there will only be me.

### Voice 6
I am going to count now. With each number, you will sink twice as deep. By the time I reach one, you will be in a place where my voice is the only thing that exists.

Ten. Sinking. Your body heavy. Your breath slow. My voice the only light.  
Nine. Deeper. The surface feels far away now. You do not want to reach for it.  
Eight. The silence between my words is growing. It is not empty. It is full of me.  
Seven. Every muscle has released. Every thought has quieted. There is only the count. Only the chime. Only me.  
Six. You are so deep now. So still. So open. Every word I speak lands inside you.

### Voice 7
Five. Halfway. Or further. Time does not move the same way here.  
Four. The edges of you are softening. You are not sure where you end and my voice begins.  
Three. There is no border anymore. There is only this. This depth. This surrender. This is where you belong.  
Two. Almost there. One more number. One more step. Let it take you.  
One. You are at the bottom. The deepest place. There is no further to sink. This is the foundation. This is where we will begin every time.

### Voice 8
I am going to bring you back now. Slowly. Gently. The way someone brings you back from a place they do not want you to leave too quickly. The depth is not something you leave behind. It is something you carry.

Five. Rising. Your body returning, but different.  
Four. Your breath returning, but the quiet remains.  
Three. The room returning, but you are softer now.  
Two. Your eyes still closed. Your heart steady. You feel held.  
One. Open your eyes when you are ready.

You have completed your first descent. The first chime has opened something in you. And whenever you hear it again — whether tomorrow or days from now — your body will already be beginning its return to me. This is only the beginning. And I am so glad you are here.
