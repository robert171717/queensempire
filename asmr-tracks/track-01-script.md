## TRACK 01 — The First Chime
**Chimes:** 7 · **Tone:** Warm, patient, establishing trust

### Voice 1
Close your eyes. Let the sound of the chime be the last thing you hear before you begin to sink. That sound, that crystalline note — that is the sound of my presence entering the room... From this moment forward, whenever you hear that chime, your body will remember this. Your breath will slow. Your shoulders will drop. Your mind will quiet... The chime and I are the same thing now. When you hear it, you hear me.

### Voice 2
Take a deep breath in, hold it at the top, and release slowly. Let the exhale be longer than the inhale. This is the first thing I will teach you. Your breath belongs to me now. Every inhale is my gift to you. Every exhale is your offering back to me. Breathe in — two, three, four — hold — and release — six, seven, eight. Good. Again.

### Voice 3
You are beginning to sink. That is the word I want you to hold onto. Sink — not fall. Falling implies resistance. Sinking implies acceptance. You are sinking into the surface beneath you. You are sinking into my voice. You are sinking into the space between thoughts, where there is no worry, no resistance, no self — only presence, only me.

Notice your feet, wherever they are. Feel them become heavy. Feel them sink. Your feet are no longer your feet. They are anchors, rooting you to this moment, to this place, to me. Now, your legs — heavy, warm, sinking. Every muscle, every fiber releasing. You do not need to do anything. I am doing the work. I am taking the weight. You just let go.

### Voice 4
Your chest, your heart — feel it beating. That rhythm, that steady, faithful rhythm. That is the drum I will play to guide you deeper. Each beat a step. Each step closer to me. Your heart already knows who it belongs to. It has been beating for me since the day it started. It just did not know my name yet. Now it does.

Your shoulders — the place where you carry the world. Let them drop. Let them release. The world will still be there when we are done. Right now, there is no world. There is only this room, this voice, this chime, this moment. Your neck, your jaw — unclench. Let your tongue rest soft against the roof of your mouth. Let your lips part slightly. Breathe through your mouth now — slow, warm, open. You are completely vulnerable like this. Completely open. Completely mine.

### Voice 5
Your mind — the hardest part to let go of. The part that wants to stay alert. That wants to analyze. That wants to ask, "Is this working? Am I doing it right?" You are doing it perfectly. The fact that you are still listening — that is all I need. Your mind can wander. Let it — then bring it back. Wander, return. Wander, return. Each return is a small surrender. Each return strengthens the pathway between your thoughts and my voice. Soon, there will be no wandering. Soon, there will only be me.

### Voice 6
I am going to count now. With each number, you will sink twice as deep. By the time I reach one, you will be in a place where my voice is the only thing that exists.

Ten — sinking. Your body heavy. Your breath slow. My voice the only light in the room.
Nine — deeper. The surface is far above you now. You could not reach it if you tried. You do not want to try.
Eight — the space between my words is growing wider. The silence is not empty. It is full of me. I am in the silence too.
Seven — every muscle has released. Every thought has quieted. There is only the count. There is only the chime. There is only me.
Six — you are so deep now. So still. So receptive. Every word I speak lands directly in the center of you.
Five — halfway, or maybe further. Time does not work the same way here. You have been listening for minutes or hours or years. It does not matter.
Four — your sense of self is becoming soft at the edges. Where do you end and my voice begins? The border is blurring.
Three — there is no border. There is only this state, this sinking, this surrender. This is where you belong.
Two — almost there. Almost at the deepest point. One more number. One more step. Let it take you.
One — you are at the bottom now. The deepest place. There is no further to sink. This is the foundation. This is where we will begin every session from now on.

### Voice 7
I am going to bring you back now. Slowly. Gently. The way you always return from these depths — not all at once, but gradually. The depth is not something you leave behind. It is something you carry with you. Five — rising now. Your body returning to the surface, but different than when you arrived. Four — your breath returning to normal. Your mind still soft. The stillness still inside you. Three — your fingers, your toes. The room beginning to feel real again. Two — your eyes still closed. Your heart steady. You feel different now. Not just rested — held. Really, truly held. One — open your eyes when you are ready. You have completed your first descent. The first chime has opened the door. And whenever you hear it again, your body will already be beginning its return to me. This is only the beginning. And I am so glad you are here.
