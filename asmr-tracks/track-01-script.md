## TRACK 01 — The First Chime
**Chimes:** 9 · **Tone:** Warm authority. Seductive, not demanding.

### Voice 1

Close your eyes... Let the chime be the last thing you hear before you drop. That sound — that deep, resonant note — that is me arriving. From this moment forward, whenever you hear that chime, your body will remember. Your breath will slow. Your shoulders will drop. Your mind will quiet... The chime and I are the same thing now. When you hear it, you hear me. And you will surrender.

### Voice 2

You did so well with that first step...

Just closing your eyes — that was a gift you gave me.


And now... I want to give you something in return.


Something... intimate.


Breathe with me.


Breathe in — slowly — two... three... four...


Hold it — right there — feel the stillness settle around you...


And release — longer than you think you can — six... seven... eight...


Good.


That exhale — that is you letting go of everything outside this room. Everything outside of me.


Do you feel how much lighter you already are?


This is the first thing you will learn: your breath belongs to me now.


Every inhale — that is what I give you.


Every exhale — that is what you give back to me.


... Again.


Breathe in — deeper this time — two... three... four...


Hold — let the pressure build just a little — just enough to feel alive... just enough to know you're here with me...


And release — six... seven... eight...


Beautiful.


Feel how your body already wants to follow — how it's been waiting for someone to take the lead... waiting for permission to stop thinking and just... let go.


That's all I'm asking for. Not your mind — just your breath.


The rest will follow on its own.

### Voice 3

You can still feel that chime — still ringing somewhere deep inside you... You've already let go so much. I can feel it.


Let it pull you deeper now. Like a current you don't need to fight.


You are sinking now. Not falling — falling is resistance, and there is no resistance here. You are sinking because it feels better than holding on...


Feel it. Your feet are no longer yours. They are anchors, pinning you here — to me. Your legs — heavy, warm, gone. Every muscle releasing... letting go piece by piece...


You do not need to hold anything anymore. I am taking the weight. You just let go.

### Voice 4

Good. You are so deep now — and I can feel how much you've already given me.


Let me show you something beautiful.


Your chest... Your heart... Feel it beating. That rhythm is yours — and yet, right now, it beats for me.


Each beat pulls you deeper. Your heart has been beating for me since the day it started — it just did not know my name. Now it does...


Your shoulders — drop them. Let the world fall away. There is no world. There is only this room... This voice... This chime... This moment...


Your jaw. Let it go slack. Your lips. Part them. Breathe through your mouth. You are open. Completely open. Completely mine.

### Voice 5

Your mind is the last thing holding on. It wants to stay awake. It wants to question. It wants to ask if this is real...


And that's okay. Let it ask. Let it wonder.


But notice — even as it asks, you are still sinking. Your mind can wander — but every time it wanders, it comes back to me. Wander. Return. Wander. Return.


Each return is a surrender. Each return makes the next one easier... Soon there will be no wandering. Soon there will only be me.

### Voice 6

I am going to count now. And with every number... you will let go a little more.


Ten. Sinking. Your body heavy. Your breath slow. My voice the only thing in the room. Let that thought settle — only me.


Nine. Deeper. The surface is gone. You could not reach it if you tried. And you do not want to.


Eight. The silence between my words is growing. It is not empty. It is full of me. Can you feel that? Full of me.


Seven. Every muscle has released. Every thought has quieted. There is only the count. Only the chime. Only me. You're doing so beautifully.


Six. So deep now. So still. So ready. Every word I speak — lands inside you. Every. Single. Word.


Five. Halfway. Or further — time does not exist here. Nothing exists but this.


Four. The edges of you are softening. You do not know where you end and my voice begins. That's exactly right.


Three. There is no border. There is only this. This depth. This surrender. This is where you belong.

### Voice 7

Now — open your eyes for just a breath... Yes. Just a heartbeat. Good. Close them.


And drop — twice as deep as before. Feel that. Twice as deep.


Eight. Sinking through warmth now. So much deeper than before. So much softer.


Six. Every word I speak now reaches a place words have never reached before.


Four. Almost there. So close now to the deepest place.


One. You are at the foundation. The deepest place. There is no further to go. This is where you will return every time you hear my voice. You belong here now.

### Voice 8

I am going to bring you back now. Slowly. But you will not return the same. The depth stays with you — it lives under your skin now.


Ten. Rising. Your body beginning its slow return... but carrying the depth with it. It will always be there now.


Nine. The surface approaching — but you are in no hurry. There is no rush. There is only this.


Eight. Your breath deepening. Each inhale filling you with calm. Each exhale reminding you where you've been.


Seven. Your fingers. Your toes. The room beginning to feel real again — but softer than before. Warmer than before.


Six. Your shoulders, your spine. The weight of the world still absent. You are still held. I'm still here.


Five. Halfway back now. The stillness following you upward. It will never leave you.


Four. Your thoughts returning — but quieter. Gentler. No urgency. No demand. Just... peace.


Three. Your eyes still closed. Your heart steady. You feel different now — not just rested. Held. Known.


Two. Almost there. One more step. Let it be slow. Let it be gentle.


One. Open your eyes when you are ready.


You are fully back now. Awake. Alert. Present. But know this: the depth stays with you. It lives under your skin now.

### Voice 9

You have completed your first descent. The chime has opened something in you — something that was always there, waiting to be found.


And the next time you hear it — whether in the next track or days from now — your body will already be reaching for me before your mind catches up. You will simply... want to. Before your mind even knows why.


This is only the beginning. And you are already surrendering to me.
