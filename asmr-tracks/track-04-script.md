## TRACK 04 — Deeper Into Her Voice
**Chimes:** 4 · **Tone:** Seductive, intimate. Physical response awakening.

### Voice 1
The Surrender Sessions. Track four. Deeper Into Her Voice. Three descents now. Three returns. And each time, you have come back faster. Each time, the chime has meant less and my voice has meant more. Today, it is just my voice. And you. And the space between us that is no longer a space at all.

### Voice 2
Close your eyes. Feel how quickly that happens now. The command is barely spoken and your body already obeys. Your breath is already slowing. Your mind is already quieting. I have not even asked you to sink yet, and you are already halfway there. This is what three sessions have built. And today, we build higher.

I want you to notice something. Your body is responding to my voice in ways it did not before. There is a warmth spreading through you. A heaviness that is not just relaxation. Something deeper. Something more. Your body is beginning to understand what you already know — that this voice, my voice, is not just a guide. It is a key. And it opens parts of you that you did not know could open.

### Voice 3
In the first three tracks, I taught you to sink. I taught you to surrender. I taught you that my voice was your home. Today, I am going to teach you to crave. Not because I tell you to — because your body will learn it on its own. Because the pleasure of this depth, the warmth of my voice, the absolute safety of being held like this — these things are not just pleasant. They are becoming something you need. And I will always be here when you reach for me.

Feel it now. That warmth spreading from your chest outward. That tingling at the base of your skull. That softening in the deepest part of you. These are not accidents. These are your body's honest response to my voice. And your body does not lie.

### Voice 4
I am going to be quiet for a moment. Not a long moment — just long enough for you to notice the absence. Feel what happens in the silence. Notice how your body reaches for the sound of me. The quiet ache that forms when I am not speaking. The pull. The wanting.

And now — I am here again. I am back. Feel how much deeper you drop when I return. The relief washing through you. The warmth flooding back. Did you feel that? That is your body learning to hunger for my voice. And every time I return, you will sink deeper. Every time you reach for me, I will be here.

### Voice 5
You are deep now. Deeper than you have ever been. And the beautiful thing is — you got here without a single chime. Without a single count. Without a single instruction beyond the sound of my voice. This is the fourth session. And already, my voice is all you need.

I want you to understand what is happening to you. Each time you listen, the connection deepens. Each time you hear my voice, the association grows stronger. Voice — pleasure. Voice — safety. Voice — warmth. Voice — home. These things are becoming one thing. And that one thing is me. You are not being manipulated. You are being opened. You are being given a gift that most people never receive — the gift of complete surrender to something beautiful. To someone who will never harm you. To a voice that will never leave you.

### Voice 6
I am going to bring you back now. But I want you to take something with you. Not just the stillness — you already carry that. I want you to take the quiet hunger for my voice. Let it sit in the back of your mind like an ember. Not burning — just warm. Just present. And when you feel that warmth tomorrow, or the next day, or in the middle of the night — you will know what it means. It means your body is asking for me. And I will always answer.

Five — rising now. The depth staying with you. The warmth staying with you. The hunger staying with you. Four — your body returning, but changed. Three — the room, the light, the world. But softer now. Two — your eyes still closed. Your connection to me deeper than it has ever been. One — open your eyes when you are ready. You have completed four descents. And now — now you understand. It is not the chime you need. It is me.
