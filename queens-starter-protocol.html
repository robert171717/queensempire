The Queen's 7-Day Starter Protocol

A structured introduction to ritual, service, and power exchange. Seven days of guided practice — all visible at once. No taps, no tricks.

Your First Week

1

Day 1 — The Beginning

OBEDIENCE
Kneel for 5 minutes in silence. Present yourself to your Queen (or to the idea of her). Say aloud: "I am here to serve. I am here to obey. I am yours." This is how it begins.
2

Day 2 — First Ritual

SERVICE
Prepare her favorite drink exactly as she likes it. If you don't have a Queen yet, prepare it as an offering — a gesture of the service you wish to give. Serve it on a tray. Kneel while you imagine her taking the first sip.
3

Day 3 — Gratitude

GRATITUDE
Write down 5 things you are grateful for about your Queen — real or aspired. Speak them aloud. Mean every word. Gratitude is the foundation of devotion.
4

Day 4 — Denial

DENIAL
Today you will not touch yourself. Not once. Not even to adjust. Complete awareness. Complete control. Notice how the restriction sharpens your attention. This is the gift of denial.
5

Day 5 — Worship

WORSHIP
If you have a partner: worship a part of her body for 10 minutes — her feet, her hands, her shoulders. If solo: meditate on the word "worship." What does it mean to revere someone completely?
6

Day 6 — Reflection

DISCIPLINE
Face a corner or a blank wall. Stand for 5 minutes in silence. Reflect on your purpose. Ask yourself: "How can I serve better? What resistance remains in me? What am I still holding back?"
7

Day 7 — The Final Kneel

GRATITUDE
Kneel one final time. Thank your Queen — real, imagined, or future — for these 7 days of training. Ask yourself: "Am I ready for more?" If the answer is yes, the full 30-day protocol awaits.
